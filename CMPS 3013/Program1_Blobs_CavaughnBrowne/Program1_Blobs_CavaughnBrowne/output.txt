Welcome Cavaughn Browne's Icky Blob Program!
This program traverses through a world filled with icky blobs
and transforms them from being made of an icky
substance '*' to a highly useful form of energy '#'.
It also counts the number of blobs it finds there 
The blobs live in a mysterious 2D Matrix World of 
which only this program can decipher from a high tech
file.
